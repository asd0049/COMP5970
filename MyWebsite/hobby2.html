My favorite book series.

The Empyrean

The Empyrean is a fantasy series by Rebecca Yarros that follows Violet Sorrengail as she trains to become a dragon rider at Basgiath War College. Here is Rebeccas Yarros Website: Rebecca Yarros

A Court of Thorns and Roses

A Court of Thorns and Roses (ACOTAR) is a young adult fantasy romance series by Sarah J. Maas. The series follows Feyre Archeron, a huntress who is kidnapped and taken to the faerie realm of Prythian.

Fable

Fable is a book where a young girl must find her place and her family while trying to survive in a world built for men.
